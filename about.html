About Dominion City Global Services

Over 20 years of excellence in industrial supply, procurement, and facility services

Our Story

Founded in 2003, Dominion City Global Services has grown to become a trusted partner for industrial companies across Oil & Gas, Manufacturing, Construction, and Facilities Management sectors. Our commitment to reliability, quality, and customer focus has made us the preferred choice for thousands of organizations seeking seamless supply chain solutions and facility services.

What started as a small regional procurement firm has evolved into a comprehensive global services provider with an extensive network of vetted suppliers, certified professionals, and cutting-edge logistics capabilities. Today, we serve major Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and emerging enterprises alike.
